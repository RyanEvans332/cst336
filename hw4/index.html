To-Do list

What is this?

This is a cookie-enabled To-Do list written by Ryan Tashiro-Evans. This time in JS and Jquery.

You can write tasks into this webpage and they will stay here even if you leave the page and come back later.

To remove tasks individually you can just hit the red 'remove' button next to the respective task

I recommend you remove all the tasks individually or clear this webpage's session when you are done toying around with it.

The cookies will remain saved to your computer for a few days or so otherwise.
