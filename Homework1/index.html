Stuxnet was a virus discovered by the infosec community in 2010. The virus itself was likely developed and deployed around 2005 and exploited several Windows zero-day vulnerabilities.

It managed to spread itself worldwide--and it spread itself fast.

But the interesting thing?: Assuming you're the average person--it was completely harmless.
